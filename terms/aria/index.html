ARIA Service Terms

These terms govern use of ARIA and the support services delivered by Integrated IT Support Inc. By subscribing, paying for, or otherwise using the service, you agree to the terms below. We keep this document plain-English on purpose — if anything is unclear, contact us before subscribing. For broader business engagement terms see /terms.

1. Service operation

ARIA delivers AI-powered IT support across web, the ARIA app, and the Chrome extension. Each plan tier defines user limits, on-site visit allotments, response SLAs, and additional features. The currently active plan governs entitlement.

2. Session timeouts & reconnect protocol

  • An idle chat or voice session automatically closes after 10 minutes of no user interaction.
  • After the session closes, ARIA will attempt to reconnect three times at 5 minutes, 15 minutes, and 45 minutes from the close.
  • Each reconnect appears as an "Incoming from ARIA" notification (flashing green) in the bottom-right of your ARIA app.
  • If all three reconnects go unanswered, the active session is dropped and the working chat is erased. The interaction record — including timestamp, attempted contacts, and original request — is retained as proof of contact and for compliance.
  • The same protocol applies if you disconnect mid-session without acknowledgement.

3. Memory, recording & continuation

  • Every interaction is recorded with timestamps and full context for compliance and audit purposes.
  • You can resume a prior interaction at any time by saying "continue from a prior issue", providing an interaction ID, or providing your first and last name. ARIA will retrieve the matching record and resume context.
  • Once an issue is resolved and no follow-up is pending, the active chat content is removed from active memory; the record of the interaction is retained.
  • If a user opens a session but does not initiate any request, ARIA discards the session entirely — no record is kept.
  • If an issue is escalated to a human technician, the interaction becomes an incident and is permanently recorded with full context, contacts, and resolution notes.

4. Hourly billing for extended interactions

Applies to all plans — including Personal and Pro Each plan includes unlimited short-form chat and voice support. A continuous, active interaction lasting more than 1 hour is billed at the prevailing hourly rate set out in your account. Any partial hour beyond the first is rounded up to the next full hour. Example: 1 hour 15 minutes of continuous active interaction = 2 hours billed.

5. On-site support

  • Personal & Pro: remote support only.
  • Small Business: includes 5 on-site visits per year.
  • Mid-Size: includes 10 on-site visits per year.
  • Enterprise: includes 24 on-site visits per year — for any request, large or small.
  • Visits beyond the included annual allotment are billed separately at the prevailing on-site rate. Travel expenses outside the standard service area are billed at cost.

6. Acquisition offers

If you are submitting an acquisition offer for ARIA via the Plans page:

  • All acquisitions retain a 5% founder stake — either 5% equity in the post-acquisition entity or 5% of ongoing revenue, at the founder's election. This is non-negotiable.
  • Qualifying offer mechanics, deposit requirements, and bid window are disclosed during the bid submission flow and require explicit acceptance at two separate consent steps before payment is processed.
  • Once both consent steps are completed, the offer is recorded as legally binding subject to the disclosed deposit and refund rules.

7. Non-payment

Failure to pay invoiced amounts — including subscription fees, hourly overages, on-site overages, or any other amounts owing — within 30 days may result in service suspension. Records and prior interactions are retained throughout any suspension.

8. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to active subscribers at least 14 days in advance via the email associated with your account.
