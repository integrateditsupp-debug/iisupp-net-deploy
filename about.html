Professional Biography

Ahmad W. did not build Integrated IT Support Inc. from the outside in — he built it from the inside out, after operating at Team Lead, Major Incident Manager, and senior technical-services levels across financial, healthcare, public-sector, legal, and global enterprise environments.

Every operational framework the company publishes is a framework its leadership has already delivered under pressure.
