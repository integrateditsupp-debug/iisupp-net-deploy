# Safe checkout — payment options ranked

## 1. Best (use these)
- **Credit card** — strongest fraud protection. Charge can be disputed, money never leaves until disputes resolve.
- **Apple Pay / Google Pay** — token-based; merchant never sees real card.
- **PayPal** — buyer protection on goods.
- **Virtual / one-time card numbers** (Privacy.com, Apple Card virtual, Capital One, etc.) — limit exposure if leaked.

## 2. Acceptable (with caution)
- **Debit card** — works but no buyer protection equivalent to credit card. Money leaves immediately.

## 3. Risky / avoid
- **Bank wire transfer** — irrevocable. Only for trusted transactions.
- **ACH push** — same.
- **Money apps to strangers** (Zelle, Cash App, Venmo to non-friends) — irreversible, no protection.

## 4. NEVER
- **Gift cards** as payment for goods/services. Always a scam.
- **Cryptocurrency** to a stranger / unknown vendor. Irreversible.
- **Western Union / MoneyGram** to a stranger. Irreversible.

## 5. At checkout — verify
- Browser padlock.
- URL matches the merchant (no typo squats).
- Total = items + tax + shipping (extras = red flag).
- Address fields prefilled correctly.
- Review final order before confirming.

## 6. After checkout
- Save confirmation email.
- Note tracking number.
- Watch for receipt that matches.

## 7. ARIA's role
- Confirm payment method choice.
- Flag if user is about to wire money or buy gift cards as payment.
- Save merchant + amount + date in user's purchase log if user wants.
