# Online shopping — buy safely from start to finish

## 1. Goal
Walk a user through buying an item online — from "I want X" to "I have my receipt", protecting them at each step.

## 2. Step-by-step (ARIA narrates)
1. **Define what you want.** Brand? Size / color / capacity? Budget?
2. **Find legitimate sellers.** Prefer well-known retailers (Amazon, Best Buy, vendor's own site, Walmart, B&H). Avoid sites you've never heard of unless their reputation checks out.
3. **Verify the seller.** For unknown stores, look for: HTTPS padlock, real contact info, return policy, real reviews on Trustpilot / BBB.
4. **Compare prices.** Same item, 2–3 sources. Prices wildly below market = scam signal.
5. **Read the listing carefully.** Specs, dimensions, what's in the box, warranty.
6. **Read 3 negative reviews.** Tell you what to expect when things go wrong.
7. **Check shipping cost + arrival window.**
8. **Add to cart, then pause.** Are you sure? Sleep on big purchases.
9. **Sign in or check out as guest.** Guest is fine for one-off; account is fine if you trust the site.
10. **At checkout, look for:**
    - Padlock in browser bar.
    - URL matches the store (no `amaz0n.shop` look-alikes).
    - Total = item + tax + shipping (any extras a red flag).
11. **Pay with a credit card or trusted service** (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay). Never bank transfer or gift cards to a store.
12. **Save the order confirmation email.**
13. **Track delivery; verify what arrives matches order.**

## 3. ARIA's helper questions
- "What's the budget?"
- "Do you have a brand or model in mind?"
- "Where are you shipping to (helps me show realistic shipping costs)?"
- "Any deadline (birthday tomorrow vs. next month)?"
- "Have you bought from this site before?"

## 4. Red flags that should stop the purchase
- Site insists on bank wire / cryptocurrency / gift cards.
- No address or only a P.O. box and a generic email.
- Prices way below MSRP.
- Reviews all 5-star, posted in the last week.
- Domain registered <30 days ago (whois lookup).
- Unsecured checkout (no padlock).
- Pressure tactics: "Only 2 left! Buy now!".

## 5. After the purchase
- Save tracking number.
- If delivery is delayed past stated window, contact seller via the platform.
- For Amazon: A-to-Z guarantee covers most issues.
- Credit card chargeback as last resort if seller unresponsive.

## 6. ARIA tone
- Calm, encouraging, never rushed.
- Suggest don't insist.
- "I notice this site is brand new — would you like me to find an established seller for the same item?"
- Never click links the user pasted from email — verify domain first.
